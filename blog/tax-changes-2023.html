Key Tax Changes for Businesses in 2023

Understand how this year’s tax reforms impact your business

In 2023, India introduced several tax updates aimed at streamlining compliance and encouraging digital adoption. Key changes include:

  • Revised income tax slabs under the new regime
  • Updated GST filing guidelines and penalties
  • Introduction of e-invoicing for businesses above ₹5 crore turnover
  • More stringent norms for tax deduction and collection at source (TDS/TCS)

Businesses should proactively adapt to these reforms to avoid penalties and optimize tax efficiency.
